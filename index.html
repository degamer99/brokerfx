    Minimum amount for investment is $300 and minimum amount for withdrawal is $2000. Maximum amount for deposit is $1,000,000 and maximum amount for withdrawal is $5,000,000. Each amount is applicable in reference to a single transaction.

  • WHAT ARE THE ACCEPTED MEANS OF DEPOSIT?

    Accepts six methods of deposit. They include 1. BITCOIN 2. LITECOIN 3. ETHEREUM 4. PERFECT MONEY 5. PAYPAL 6. BITCOIN CASH.

  • HOW CAN I DEPOSITE MONEY?

    That all deposits/payments should be made directly to the company account/ bitcoin wallet address, no payments/deposits should be made to any account manager. The company will not be held responsible for any loss that comes with making payments to any account’s manager.

  • HOW CAN I DO A WITHDRAWAL?

    Withdrawals are processed instantly. However, Bitcoin transactions might take a while for full confirmation from blockchain. But withdrawal through other means are processed Instantly and securely.
